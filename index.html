Hi, I'm Peter.

I am a full time student, doing a Master of Science in Electronic Systems Design in Trondheim, Norway. I am also a part time employee at Norbit Subsea, where I am a project engineer. I use a lot of my spare time tinkering, playing with home automation and you bet I always have a project going.

Some of my work

Toilet Paper Roll Counter Box

A box to count the number of toilet paper rolls and reporting it to Home Assistant so we're never out of toilet paper

Video Doorbell

A cheap video doorbell for Home Assistant with a 3D printed casing to monitor our front door when we have guests coming over.

Solder Station

A simple soldering station made smart, so that when you lift the soldering iron off its dock, it turns on and the solder fan and light comes on.

AUX Switcher

A box that automates the switching between PC speakers and headphones.

Table Fan

A cheap table fan that can be fully automated for warm summer days.

OV Door Status

A xbar plugin that checks the Omega Verksteds door to see if they're open or not.

HA Fetch

A xbar plugin that can show you sensor state from Home Assistant.

If you want to check out more of my work and follow along more closely, check out my GitHub as this is where I post most of my projects.

NTNU related

TFE4580 Project thesis

The fall of 2020 I wrote a project thesis, where I took a deeper look into realism in AR and tried to figure out what the level of occlusion had to say for the Quality of Experience of the user in an augmented reality system

Elektronikk- & Teknologidagen 2019

Elektronikk- & Teknologidagen is a yearly career fair organized by the electronic engineering students. In 2019 I was leading the division for company recruiting. We delivered a substantial growth, with a 30% income increase compared to years before us. This made the following excursion to Asia practically free for the students.
